<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Demo Walkthrough">

To make the most of your time in the **rise of the ronin demo**, follow this optimized path to ensure you unlock all transferrable rewards and master the mechanics before the final boss.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Character Creation and Origin">
    Spend time in the character creator for both twins. Your "Origin" (class) determines your starting stats and special skills. The "Slayer" origin is recommended for beginners due to high initial Strength.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Veiled Edge Tutorial">
    Don't skip the combat trials. Practice the Counterspark against the training master until you can land three consecutive parries. This skill is non-negotiable for the upcoming boss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Infiltrating the Black Ships">
    Use the grappling hook to stay on the masts and rooftops. Stealth-kill the sentries to avoid being overwhelmed by riflemen. Collect the "Rusty Key" from the captain's quarters to progress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Boss Duel">
    Face off against the American officer. Focus on his Ki bar rather than his health. Once his Ki is depleted (turns red), use a Critical Hit to deal massive damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Technical Performance and Settings">

The **rise of the ronin demo** offers three distinct graphical modes on the PlayStation 5. Choosing the right one is crucial for a game that relies so heavily on parry timings.

| Mode | Target Resolution | Target FPS | Features |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Performance** | 1440p (Upscaled) | 60 FPS | Stable frame rate, lower shadows |
| **Graphics** | Native 4K | 30 FPS | High-res textures, better lighting |
| **Ray Tracing** | 1440p (Dynamic) | 30 FPS | Realistic reflections and shadows |

<Callout type="info" title="Expert Recommendation">
We strongly recommend **Performance Mode**. The 60 FPS target provides a much tighter window for Countersparks, making the combat feel significantly more responsive.
</Callout>

### Key Settings to Adjust

- **Motion Blur**: Set to "Low" or "Off" to increase clarity during fast rotations.
- **Camera Sensitivity**: Increase to 6 or 7 to track enemies who dash behind you.
- **HUD Scaling**: Use the "Minimalist" setting to enjoy the beautiful Bakumatsu scenery without clutter.
