<Section icon={Swords} title="Dojo PvP and Hardcore Combat Modes">

Rise of the Ronin features one of the most sophisticated melee combat systems in modern gaming, blending parries, stance-switching, and martial skills. While the base game supports cooperative play for missions, it lacks a dedicated player-versus-player (PvP) mode. The existing Dojo challenges provide the perfect foundation for competitive matchmaking.

| Mode Format | Player Count | Combat Restrictions | Reward Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dojo Duel (Ranked)** | 1v1 | Practice wooden weapons, limited healing | ★★★★★ (Legendary Gear) |
| **Skirmish Brawl** | 2v2 or 3v3 | Full custom loadouts, active martial arts | ★★★★☆ (Master Crafting Mats) |
| **Hardcore Sudden Death** | 1v1 | 2-3 hit elimination, no healing items | ★★★★★ (Exclusive Title & Skins) |
| **Dojo Tournament** | 4-Player Bracket | Sequential matches, stamina carryover | ★★★★★ (Unique Stance Scrolls) |

Implementing a PvP system within the Dojo would satisfy the hardcore community's demand for competitive combat. By limiting healing items and utilizing the practice weapons already featured in Dojo trials, Team Ninja could create a balanced environment where player skill, timing, and stance mastery dictate the victor.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master Counterspark Timing">
    Practice parrying rapid attack strings against high-level Dojo bosses like Ryoma Sakamoto or Toshizo Hijikata. Perfecting your Counterspark is essential for disrupting opponents in PvP.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Ki Consumption Gear">
    Equip armor sets that reduce Ki consumption during dodges and attacks. In competitive duels, running out of Ki leaves you completely vulnerable to devastating critical hits.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Diverse Combat Stances">
    Collect and upgrade multiple combat stances (Ten, Chi, and Jin) for your primary weapons. Being able to switch stances mid-combo allows you to bypass an opponent's defensive advantages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
