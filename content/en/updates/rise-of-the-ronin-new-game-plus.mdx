<Section icon={Target} title="Midnight Difficulty and World Progression">

The defining feature of the **rise of the ronin new game plus** is the Midnight difficulty setting. This is not simply a "hard mode" but a complete restructuring of the game's challenge level. Accessed via the Testament of the Soul in your Longhouse, Midnight difficulty resets the world state while allowing you to keep all your gear, levels, and skills.

In Midnight mode, the level floor for enemies starts at 60. Even if you finished the campaign at level 45 or 50, you will find yourself significantly underleveled for the initial encounters in Yokohama. The progression is gated by region, requiring you to complete specific Ronin missions to move from Yokohama to Edo, and eventually to Kyoto.

| Feature | Dusk (Standard) | Midnight (NG+) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Enemy Level** | Scales with Story | Level 60 to Level 100+ |
| **Loot Rarity** | Up to Legendary (Gold) | Masterwork (Green) Unlocked |
| **Enemy Aggression** | Standard AI | Faster attacks, less recovery time |
| **Public Orders** | Fixed completion | Respawned for new rewards |
| **Fugitives** | Fixed completion | Respawned with Masterwork drops |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Testament">
    Enter your Longhouse and interact with the Testament of the Soul. Look for the prompt in the bottom right corner to switch the world state to Midnight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Yokohama Missions">
    You must complete a set number of Ronin (Main) missions in the Yokohama region. Focus on these to build your initial Masterwork set.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Edo and Kyoto">

</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Masterwork Equipment and Crafting Upgrades">

Masterwork equipment represents the pinnacle of gear in **rise of the ronin new game plus**. Identified by its distinct green icon, Masterwork gear offers higher base stats and more slots for special effects compared to Legendary (Gold) items. These items only drop in Midnight difficulty or as rewards for high-level Ally missions and secret boss refights.

The Longhouse also receives a significant upgrade in the post-game. You can now access full blacksmithing and crafting services directly from your home base, removing the need to travel to specific vendors in the city. This streamlines the process of Bond Transferring and upgrading your new Masterwork find.

| Gear Aspect | Legendary (Gold) | Masterwork (Green) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Damage/Defense** | High | Maximum |
| **Special Effect Slots** | 3-4 Slots | 4-5 Slots |
| **Upgrade Potential** | Limited by standard mats | Requires Masterwork materials |
| **Drop Source** | All difficulties | Midnight Difficulty only |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Breakthrough Skills and Combat Mastery">

Beyond the standard skill trees (Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, Charisma), the **rise of the ronin new game plus** introduces Breakthrough Skills. These are found in the stats menu, marked by the "Veiled Edge" symbol. Breakthrough Skills allow for infinite (or very high cap) progression in core character attributes.

These skills are essential for surviving the endgame, as they provide the raw power needed to overcome the level 60+ enemies. You will earn Breakthrough points by gaining experience at level cap or completing specific Midnight-only objectives.

| Skill Category | Primary Benefit | Scaling Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Health Mastery** | Increases Maximum HP | Flat bonus per level |
| **Weapon Mastery** | Increases Attack Power | Percentage based |
| **Sub-Weapon Skill** | Boosts Bow/Rifle/Shuriken | Damage and Reload speed |
| **Combat Styles** | Unlocks new style variants | Unique move sets |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Replayability and Secret Bosses">

The Testament of the Soul is not just for changing difficulty; it is the key to exploring the "What If" scenarios of the story. In **rise of the ronin new game plus**, you can go back to pivotal moments and choose the opposite faction (Pro-Shogunate vs. Anti-Shogunate) to see how the world changes and unlock New Missions that were previously inaccessible.

Additionally, the world contains hidden "Secret Bosses"—powerful warriors tucked away in remote corners of the map. In Midnight difficulty, these bosses are tuned for level 100 encounters and drop some of the most powerful Masterwork items in the game.

| Activity | Reward Type | Replayability Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Secret Bosses** | Unique Masterwork Sets | High (Best in slot gear) |
| **Bond Missions** | Companion Masterwork Gear | Medium (Build specific) |
| **Public Orders** | Consumables and Materials | Low (Farming only) |
