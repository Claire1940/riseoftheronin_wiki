<Section icon={Target} title="Combat Styles & Counterspark Mastery">

Combat in **rise of the ronin ps5** revolves around the dynamic interaction between different combat styles and the **Counterspark** parry mechanic. Combat styles are divided into three primary categories: **Ten** (Sky), **Chi** (Earth), and **Jin** (Human). There are also specialized **Shinobi** styles that offer unique, unconventional movesets.

Understanding the relationship between these styles is critical. An advantageous matchup increases the stagger and Ki damage you inflict, while a disadvantaged matchup makes deflecting enemy strikes significantly harder.

| Style | Meaning | Effective Against | Weak Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ten** | Sky | Katanas and medium weapons | Sabers and light weapons |
| **Chi** | Earth | Odachis and heavy weapons | Katanas and medium weapons |
| **Jin** | Human | Sabers and light weapons | Odachis and heavy weapons |
| **Shinobi** | Specialist | Varies by opponent type | Standard defensive styles |

To master the flow of battle, you must learn to manage your Ki and execute defensive maneuvers sequentially. Use the following steps to build a reliable defensive routine.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Observe and Block First">
    Do not attempt to parry every strike immediately. Hold the guard button to study the enemy's attack rhythm and identify the final hit of their combination.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Time the Counterspark">
    Press the Triangle button shortly before an attack connects. Focus on the physical weapon's movement rather than the enemy's body transitions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Perform a Blade Flash">
    After landing a successful combination, press the R1 button to perform a Blade Flash. This cleans blood from your weapon and instantly restores a portion of your spent Ki.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Martial Skills">
    Once an enemy's Ki is depleted and they enter a panicked state, execute a high-damage Martial Skill to break their stance and set up a devastating Critical Hit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="The Power of Blade Flash">
Consistently using Blade Flash between attack strings is the key to maintaining offensive momentum. It prevents you from running out of Ki and being left temporarily defenseless.
</Callout>

As you earn experience and complete missions throughout Yokohama, Edo, and Kyoto, you will accumulate skill points to invest across four primary attribute trees. Each tree enhances specific combat capabilities, secondary tools, and world interactions.

To build an effective character, align your attribute investments with your preferred weapon choices and playstyle.

| Attribute | Core Focus | Key Weapons | Secondary Benefits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Strength** | Direct damage & defense | Katana, Odachi, Greatsword | Improved guard efficiency |

| **Charm** | Ki recovery & luck | Katana, Polearm, Oxtail | Better merchant discounts |

For players who prefer a versatile approach, the **Balanced Ronin** build distributes points evenly across all four trees. This unlocks essential survival skills, basic stealth takedowns, and helpful dialogue options without overcommitting to a single specialization early on.

<Callout type="info" title="Respeccing Skill Points">
