<Section icon={Target} title="Beginner Progression & Gameplay Steps">

Starting your journey as a masterless samurai can be daunting. The open world is filled with rival factions, dangerous fugitives, and complex political choices. Follow this structured progression path to establish a strong foothold during your first few hours in Yokohama.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Balanced Difficulty">
    Choose **Dawn** if you want to focus on the story with more forgiving recovery items and lower Ki consumption. Choose **Dusk** for the standard, parry-focused action-RPG balance. You can adjust this setting at any time from the menu or before entering major story missions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Practice Counterspark Timing">
    The Counterspark serves as both an attack and a parry. Do not react to the enemy's initial body movement; instead, watch their weapon and press the Counterspark button shortly before the strike connects. If an enemy uses a complex combination, block the first few hits and Counterspark the final blow.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Manage Your Blood Gauge with Blade Flash">
    As you attack, blood accumulates on your weapon, filling the blood gauge. Pressing the Blade Flash button immediately after a combination cleans the blade, restoring a significant portion of your spent Ki. Consistently using Blade Flash prevents you from being left vulnerable with an empty stamina bar.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Establish Region and Character Bonds">
    Explore Yokohama to clear bandit-controlled areas and restore Public Order. Doing so increases your Region Bond, unlocking merchant discounts and revealing hidden collectibles. Talk to companion characters, complete their unique Bond Missions, and offer them gifts to unlock exclusive combat styles and skills.
  </Step>
</Steps>
