<Section icon={Map} title="Steam Co-op Setup & Online Matchmaking Guide">

The PC version supports seamless online cooperative play for up to three players. Matchmaking is handled directly through Steam's networking infrastructure, meaning players do not need external subscription services to access online features.

| Feature Type | How It Works | Gameplay Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Direct Co-op | Join or recruit up to 3 players for specific story missions | Shared combat, cooperative boss fights |
| Password Match | Restrict your lobby to friends using a matching key | Private sessions without random matchmaking |
| Async Ronin | Other players' avatars appear as NPCs in your world | Fight them for loot or recruit them as AI allies |
| Pilgrim Dog | Send your dog to other worlds with deposited money | Returns later with rare items and materials |

To host a session, select "Recruit" from a supported mission's cooperative setup screen. If you wish to join a specific friend, ensure both of you have set the exact same password in the matchmaking settings.

<Callout type="info" title="Co-op Limitations">
Cooperative play is strictly mission-based. You cannot freely roam the open world with friends; once a co-op mission is completed, players will return to their respective solo worlds.
