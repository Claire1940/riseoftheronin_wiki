<Section icon={Map} title="How to Set Up Co-op Multiplayer">

Setting up cooperative play in the game requires reaching a specific milestone in the main story. Once you unlock the Longhouse (Nagaya) in Yokohama, the cooperative features become available. This hub serves as your central command for organizing multiplayer sessions with friends or random players.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Longhouse">
    Progress through the prologue and early story chapters until you secure your personal Longhouse in Yokohama.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Access the Cooperate Menu">
    Interact with the journal inside the Longhouse and select the Cooperate option to open the multiplayer lobby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Configure Matchmaking Settings">
    Set your lobby preferences, including password protection, region locks, and difficulty parameters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Invite Allies or Join Lobbies">
    Send direct invitations to players on your friends list or search for active public lobbies matching your criteria.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Adjusting your matchmaking settings ensures a stable connection and aligns your gameplay expectations with your partners. The table below highlights the primary settings available in the cooperative menu.

|Setting|Options|Purpose|Recommendation|
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
|**Lobby Type**|Public / Private|Restrict entry|Private for friends|
|**Passcode**|4-Digit Number|Direct matching|Use for co-op|
|**Region Lock**|On / Off|Latency control|Off for faster matching|
|**Difficulty**|Dawn / Dusk / Twilight|Set combat challenge|Match host preference|
