<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Combat Mechanics and Ki Management">

Combat in Rise of the Ronin is fundamentally a battle of stamina, known as Ki. Every action—swinging your weapon, blocking, or dodging—expends Ki. If your meter is depleted, you enter a "winded" state, leaving you vulnerable to critical strikes. Therefore, managing your Ki while pressuring the enemy's meter is the core loop of high-level play.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Blade Flash">
    After a series of attacks, blood accumulates on your weapon. Press **R1** immediately after an attack to perform a Blade Flash. This cleans the blade and restores a portion of your Ki based on the blood gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Counterspark">
    This is your primary defensive tool. Unlike blocking, which still costs Ki, a successful Counterspark (parry) restores Ki and panics the enemy. Look for the **red flash** on enemy Marshall Skills to time your parry.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Flash Attacks">
    Unlock this skill early in the Strength tree. By pressing **R1 + D-Pad Up** mid-combo, you swap to your secondary weapon and continue the attack. This fills the blood gauge of the new weapon instantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Violent Gale">
    Available at weapon proficiency level five, this allows you to swap stances (Ten/Chi/Jin) mid-combo. Use this to adapt to an enemy who changes their own stance without stopping your offensive flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combo Efficiency">
Combining Blade Flash with Flash Attacks allows you to extend your combos indefinitely, provided you maintain a rhythm that cleans your blade regularly.
</Callout>

</Section>

Mastering the battlefield requires more than just knowing your **rise of the ronin weapon types**; you must also understand the status effects you can inflict and suffer. Inflicting multiple status effects simultaneously leads to the devastating **Dizzy** state.

| Status Effect | Description | Counter / Remedy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Burn** | Continuous health drain | Dodge repeatedly to extinguish |
| **Poison** | Slow health drain over time | Use Antidote items |
| **Paralysis** | Freezes the character in place | Wait for duration or use remedy |
| **Flammable** | Increases susceptibility to Burn | Avoid fire sources while active |
| **Dizzy** | Occurs when 2+ effects are active | Maximum Ki damage and Panic state |

You can apply these effects using whetstones on your primary weapons or by using specific sub-weapons like the Fire Pipe. In missions where you have allies, swapping between characters can help manage these effects, as the AI-controlled companions can distract enemies while you recover.
