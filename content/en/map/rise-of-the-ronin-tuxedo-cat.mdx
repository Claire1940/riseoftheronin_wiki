<Section icon={Target} title="How to Capture Skittish Tuxedo Cats">

Capturing a **rise of the ronin tuxedo cat** requires more than just walking up to it. Some cats are "Skittish," meaning they will flee if they sense your presence. If a cat disappears, you must fast-travel away and return later to try again.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Breed">
    Observe the cat from a distance. Tuxedo cats are black with white chests and paws. If the cat's ears are twitching, it is likely a skittish type.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter Stealth Mode">
    Crouch by pressing the designated stealth button. Approach from the rear or side to stay out of the cat's direct line of sight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Use the Environment">
    If the cat is on a rooftop, use your Grappling Hook to reach a nearby ledge, then glide down silently behind the target.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute the Rescue">
    Wait for the 'Pet' prompt to appear. Once you press the button, a short animation plays, and the cat is officially added to your collection.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Collection Benefit">
Every cat you rescue increases your 'Cat Discovery' rank, which directly improves the quality of items brought back by the Cat Concierge missions.
</Callout>

</Section>
