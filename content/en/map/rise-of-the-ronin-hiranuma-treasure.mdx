<Section icon={Map} title="Hiranuma District: Yokohama Exploration Overview">

The Hiranuma district serves as a vital hub during your first few hours in Yokohama. As a Ronin navigating the Bakumatsu era, your ability to secure the **Rise of the Ronin Hiranuma treasure** will directly influence your combat effectiveness. This area is characterized by its mix of residential streets, riverfront paths, and bandit-occupied warehouses. Unlike the more open rural areas, Hiranuma requires a vertical approach to exploration.

Treasure hunting in this district is not merely about gold; it is about securing the specialized gear needed to survive the escalating difficulty of the Shogunate's forces. The chests in Hiranuma often contain superior versions of the standard Katana or Paired Swords, along with the "Intellect" or "Strength" materials required for early-game skill tree progression.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Clearing Public Order for Treasure Access">

To maximize your efficiency in Hiranuma, you must prioritize the restoration of Public Order. Hostile encampments suppress the local map, hiding the exact coordinates of the **Rise of the Ronin Hiranuma treasure**. Clearing these areas provides a triple benefit: immediate loot from the chest, a permanent increase in Region Bond, and the return of local merchants who may sell rare upgrade components.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Scout the Perimeter">
    Use your camera or high vantage points to mark enemies. Identify the leader (marked with a red icon) as they usually hold the keys to locked treasure containers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Silent Takedowns">
    Use the rooftops to perform assassinations on isolated sentries. This reduces the number of enemies you have to fight simultaneously during the final confrontation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Engage the Leader">
    Once the fodder is cleared, engage the leader. Use the **Jin** style if they carry a saber or the **Chi** style if they use an Odachi to maximize Ki pressure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Loot and Restore">
    After the "Public Order Restored" message appears, the treasure location will be highlighted. Search the central building or the leader's tent for the primary chest.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Success Tip">
Restoring Public Order in Hiranuma often triggers a "Veiled Edge Banner" activation, allowing you to fast-travel and replenish your healing items immediately after the fight.
</Callout>

</Section>
