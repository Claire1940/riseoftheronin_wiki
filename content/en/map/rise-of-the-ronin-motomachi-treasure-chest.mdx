Finding the physical location of the **rise of the ronin motomachi treasure chest** is only half the battle. The chest itself is securely locked inside a traditional Japanese storehouse (*kura*) located in the southern portion of the Motomachi district. This storehouse features distinctive white-plastered walls and heavy wooden doors that cannot be breached by normal attacks or explosives.

To reach the storehouse, start at the Motomachi Veiled Edge Banner and head south toward the coastline. The building is nestled near a cluster of residential structures, surrounded by wooden fences and patrolled by a few stray hostile guards.

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Unlock the Motomachi Storehouse">

Unlocking the storehouse requires you to confront the bandits occupying the central camp in Motomachi. This Public Order event must be completely resolved to spawn the key holder. 

When engaging the bandits, remember to manage your Ki and use your Countersparks effectively. If you face multiple enemies, use stealth to thin their ranks before engaging in direct combat.

| Enemy Type | Primary Weapon | Vulnerable Style Matchup | Threat Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Bandit Scout | Saber | Jin Style | Low |
| Camp Guard | Spear | Ten Style | Medium |
| Bandit Leader | Odachi | Chi Style | High |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Infiltrate the Bandit Camp">
    Approach the central Motomachi Public Order zone from the rooftops to maintain a stealth advantage. Use your bow or a silent assassination to eliminate the high-ground scouts first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat the Bandit Leader">
    Locate the elite Bandit Leader wielding a heavy Odachi. Engage him using the Chi combat style to counter his heavy attacks. Time your Countersparks to deplete his Ki and execute a Critical Hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Loot the Rusty Key">
    Once the Bandit Leader is defeated and the Public Order is restored, approach his body to loot the Rusty Key. If it does not auto-loot, check the small wooden crate located in the center of the cleared camp.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unlock the Storehouse Door">
    Return to the southern storehouse with the Rusty Key in your inventory. Interact with the locked wooden door to trigger the unlock animation and gain access to the interior.
  </Step>
</Steps>
