<Section icon={Shield} title="Restoring Public Order and Hunting Fugitives">

Many areas of the **rise of the ronin map** begin in a state of unrest, marked by red zones. In these areas, Public Order has deteriorated due to bandit activity or factional disputes. Restoring order is the most effective way to unlock the full potential of a district.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate the Unrest Zone">
    Identify red-shaded areas on your map. These indicate locations where bandits or rogue samurai have taken control of a village or outpost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Eliminate the Leaders">
    Enter the zone and look for enemies with a white icon above their heads. These are the leaders. Defeating them immediately breaks the enemy's morale.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Defeat Named Fugitives">
    High-value targets often hide within these zones. Fugitives are significantly tougher than standard enemies and require precise Counterspark timing to defeat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Raise the Banner">
    Once all threats are neutralized, interact with the local Veiled Edge Banner to restore order. This returns civilians to the area and reveals all nearby collectibles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Region Bonds and Progression Rewards">

As you interact with the **rise of the ronin map**, you develop a **Region Bond**. This represents your influence and reputation within that specific area. There are three levels of Region Bond, each offering progressively better benefits.

| Bond Level | Requirement | Unlockable Benefits |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Level 1** | Restore Public Order | Basic map icons revealed, local merchant access |
| **Level 2** | Complete side tasks | Collectible locations revealed, merchant discounts |
| **Level 3** | 100% District activity | Rare item availability, maximum fast travel speed |

Beyond just revealing icons, high Region Bonds allow you to purchase district-specific gear that cannot be found elsewhere. For example, the foreign settlement in Yokohama offers Western-style sabers and firearms that are unavailable in the more traditional rural districts.

<Callout type="info" title="Map Sync">
In the late game, you can use **Testament of the Soul** to sync your level to a region. This makes low-level map areas challenging again and allows you to farm high-tier endgame materials from early-game fugitives.
</Callout>

</Section>
