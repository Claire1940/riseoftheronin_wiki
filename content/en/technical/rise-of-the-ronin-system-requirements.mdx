The most striking aspect of the rise of the ronin system requirements is the 180GB SSD storage requirement. This massive footprint is driven by high-resolution textures, detailed open-world environments, and fast asset streaming mechanics. To maintain stable performance, players must optimize their storage devices and system memory.

Multi-channel memory architecture is strongly recommended for this title. Using a single stick of 16GB RAM (single-channel) can limit CPU bandwidth, leading to sudden frame drops when moving quickly through the game world. Utilizing dual-channel RAM (such as two 8GB sticks) provides the necessary bandwidth to keep framerates steady.

| Storage Device | Read Speed | Performance Impact | Recommendation |
