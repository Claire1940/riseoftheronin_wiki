<Section icon={Save} title="In-Game Save Mechanics and Methods">

Understanding the **rise of the ronin save location** starts with mastering the in-game mechanics. Unlike traditional linear titles, this open-world RPG utilizes a hybrid system of auto-saves, manual checkpoints, and physical world interactions. The game frequently auto-saves after major events, such as completing a quest, defeating a boss, or fast-traveling. However, relying solely on auto-save can be risky before tackling high-difficulty areas.

Manual saving is your most reliable tool for preserving progress. You can access this feature by opening the main menu, navigating to the "System" tab, and selecting "Save." This creates a permanent save slot that will not be overwritten by the auto-save system.

<Section icon={HardDrive} title="Technical Save Data Location on PS5">

If you are looking for the physical **rise of the ronin save location** to manage your storage or troubleshoot corrupted data, you must navigate the PlayStation 5 system menus. The game does not store saves within its own application folder but rather in the dedicated "Saved Data" partition of your SSD.

Managing this data is vital if you plan to transfer your progress to another console or if you need to delete local files to sync with a fresh Cloud Save.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access System Settings">
    From the PS5 home screen, navigate to the **Settings** gear icon in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to Storage">
    Scroll down to the **Storage** menu and select it to view your console's drive health and data distribution.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select Saved Data">
    Choose **Saved Data** from the list, then select **PS5 Games**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Locate Rise of the Ronin">
    Find **Rise of the Ronin** in the list of installed games. Here you can see the total size of your save files and individual save slots.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Data Management">
Be extremely careful when deleting files in this menu. Deleting the "Console Storage" version without a "Cloud Storage" backup will result in permanent loss of all character progress, including your gear and story completion.
</Callout>

| Data Type | Location | Backup Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Local Save** | PS5 Internal SSD | Cloud Sync / USB (PS4 only) |
| **Cloud Save** | PSN Servers | Automatic (Requires PS Plus) |
| **User Settings** | System Storage | Linked to User Profile |

<Section icon={Target} title="Backup and Troubleshooting Guide">

Maintaining the integrity of your **rise of the ronin save location** is essential for a smooth 100-hour playthrough. Occasionally, players may encounter "Save Data Mismatch" errors, especially when switching between offline and online play. To prevent this, ensure your console is always updated to the latest firmware before starting a session.

If you find that your manual saves are not appearing, check the "Load Game" menu specifically. The "Continue" option only loads the most recent save (which is often an auto-save), whereas "Load Game" allows you to pick from your specific manual timestamps.
