<Section icon={Map} title="How to Change Your Choice Using Testament of the Soul">

If you regret your decision, the game provides a mechanic to rewrite history. The Testament of the Soul, accessed from your Longhouse, allows you to replay completed missions and alter key decisions.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Longhouse">
    Travel to any major city and enter your personal Longhouse.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Open the Testament of the Soul">
    Interact with the book icon to open the timeline interface.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Select the Sakurada Gate Incident">
    Navigate to the Chapter 1 mission list and select the Sakurada Gate Incident.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Replay and Choose the Alternative">
    Complete the mission boss fight and choose the opposite option at the prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Reflect the Changes to the Timeline">
    Choose to apply the new decisions to your current world state when prompted at the end of the mission.
  </Step>
</Steps>
