### Relationship Rewards
Building bonds with your companions unlocks the **Veiled Vow** (romance) trophies. You must reach the highest bond level with at least one eligible character.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Endgame: Midnight Difficulty and Cleanup">

The final hurdle in your **rise of the ronin trophy guide** is the post-game content. After completing the main story, you unlock the Midnight difficulty level. This mode features tougher enemies and better loot drops.

1. **Midnight Crossing**: Complete any mission on Midnight difficulty. The easiest way is to replay the first mission of the prologue using your end-game gear.
2. **Level Mastery**: Reach Level 55. If you have cleared all regions, you should be close to this. If not, replaying missions on Midnight provides massive XP.
3. **Equipment Upgrades**: Enhance a piece of equipment to its maximum level. Save your materials for a "Masterwork" (red) rarity item found in the post-game.

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning">
Upgrading gear is expensive. Only invest heavily in weapons that have four compatible set bonuses to maximize your efficiency in combat.
</Callout>
