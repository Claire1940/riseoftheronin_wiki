Understanding **rise of the ronin how to get into military academy** requires a clear grasp of the game's regional progression. The Military Academy, or Kobusho, is not available during your initial hours in Yokohama. Instead, it serves as a central hub for the second major act of the game. Players must first resolve the primary conflicts in Yokohama, including the encounter with Gonzo and the infiltration of the Black Ships, before the narrative shifts toward the Shogunate's political heart in Edo.

Once you arrive in Edo, the world opens up significantly. The Kanda district becomes your primary destination for martial advancement. The Kobusho is more than just a story landmark; it is a functional training ground where the ronin can test their skills against established allies and rivals. This transition marks a shift from survival-based exploration to refined martial mastery.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Training and Dojo Mechanics">

The primary reason players seek the Military Academy is the Dojo. This system allows you to engage in controlled duels with characters you have bonded with throughout the story. Unlike open-world encounters, Dojo fights are ranked and offer specific rewards based on your performance. Mastering these duels is essential for unlocking advanced tiers of combat styles like the **Nioh-ryu** or **Aisu Kage-ryu**.

Each duel is evaluated on three criteria: Time Elapsed, Health Remaining, and Technical Execution (Countersparks and Ki management). Achieving a "Master" rank requires a deep understanding of the Ten, Chi, and Jin relationship.

| Training Rank | Score Requirement | Rewards Provided |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Novice** | 0 - 3,000 | Basic Consumables / Copper Coins |
| **Intermediate** | 3,001 - 6,000 | Rare Crafting Materials / Skill Points |
| **Advanced** | 6,001 - 8,500 | High-Tier Equipment / Style Unlock |
| **Master** | 8,501+ | **Legendary Gear / Master Style Skill** |

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="NPC Bonds and Academy Rewards">

The Military Academy serves as a social hub for several key characters. Building bonds here is just as important as the combat itself. By interacting with the NPCs located within the Kobusho, you can unlock unique dialogue, gifts, and even romance options through the **Veiled Vow** system. Characters like **Jigoro Kano**, the founder of Judo, provide unique perspectives on the evolution of Japanese martial arts during the Bakumatsu period.

Raising your Bond Level with Academy regulars provides passive bonuses that affect your entire playthrough, including increased Ki recovery and better loot drop rates from human enemies.
