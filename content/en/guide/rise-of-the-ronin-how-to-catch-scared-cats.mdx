<Section icon={Target} title="Rise of the Ronin How to Catch Scared Cats: Step-by-Step Stealth">

To successfully execute the **rise of the ronin how to catch scared cats** technique, you must master the game's stealth mechanics. Unlike combat stealth, where you aim for a critical strike, cat stealth requires a gentle touch. If you move too quickly, the cat will hiss and vanish in a puff of smoke, forcing you to wait for it to respawn.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Locate and Identify">
    Find a cat icon on your map. As you approach, look for the yellow "caution" gauge above the cat. If the icon is present, it is a scared cat.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enter Stealth Mode">
    Press **L3 (Left Stick)** to crouch. This reduces your noise profile significantly and lowers your visibility.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="The Slow Approach">
    Move the analog stick gently. Do not tilt it fully. Watch the detection meter; if it begins to fill, stop moving entirely until it recedes.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Final Interaction">
    Once you are within arm's reach (the prompt will change to 'Pet'), press the interaction button immediately to secure the feline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Avoid Jumping">
Jumping or using the grappling hook too close to a scared cat will instantly trigger its flight response. Always stay grounded during the final 10 meters of your approach.
</Callout>

| Approach Phase | Movement Speed | Detection Risk | Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Initial Sight** | Jogging | Low | Identify if it is a scared type |
| **20 Meters** | Walking | Medium | Press L3 to enter crouch |
| **10 Meters** | Slow Creep | High | Move stick only halfway |
| **Contact** | Stationary | Critical | Press Triangle/Interact |

</Section>
